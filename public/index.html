The Problem

Medication Errors
Cost Lives.

Many individuals struggle to follow their prescribed medication schedules. Missed doses, delayed intake, and confusion between medicines are common, particularly among elderly users and those managing multiple medications. These challenges can lead to health risks, reduced treatment effectiveness, and increased stress for patients and caregivers. SmartMediKit addresses these issues by providing a structured, time-aware solution that actively guides users through their daily medication routine.

The Workflow

Seamlessly integrating into your daily routine with zero friction.

01

Step 1: Day-Based Organization

Medication is organized into clearly labeled daily compartments according to the days of the week.

02

Step 2: Medication Setup

The user or caregiver fills the sub-compartments and sets the dose schedule using the intuitive onboard interface.

03

Step 3: Daily Smart Lock Activation

The system activates the smart locking mechanism, securing all compartments to prevent accidental double dosing.

04

Step 4: Time-Based Alerts

The built-in alarm system sounds at the exact scheduled time to notify the user that medication is due.

05

Step 5: Color-Guided Access

The indicator light for the active compartment turns green and the lock releases, while others remain red and secured.

06

Step 6: Dose Confirmation and Monitoring

The system monitors if the compartment was opened, providing a clear visual record of intake for the user or caregiver.

07

Step 7: Automatic Locking and Daily Reset

The compartment relocks automatically after use, and the system prepares the schedule for the next dosage cycle.
